------- Who are we? -------

Neema Technologies is a dealer of milk ATMs, salad ATMs, kerosene ATMs, Water purifiers, Domestic water purifiers,automatic hand washing machine and water vending machines.

We are experienced in this sector having supplied over 200 plus machines to happy clients across the country. Our commitment is providing excellent services to the customers who are our backbone.

The machines are manufactured from food grade equipment to ensure that they are fit to handle human food. We deliver the machines to any part of the country and offering the best after sales services so that your machine remains operational in case of any breakdown.

The machines also come with one year warranty to give you the peace of mind through our commitment to provide the best machines.

The machines come in different capacities to allow you the best fit for your budget. All the machines also support for the coin acceptors controllers. In the case of Milk ATMs they are approved by the Dairy Board.
