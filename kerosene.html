~Getting Licensed To Run The Kerosene Vending Shop~

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

There are no major license requirements for running a small kerosene outlet.

The only license required to start is the Single Business Permit which is issued by the County Government.

Those in Nairobi County may also be required to acquire a fire clearance certificate.

Generally to be in good books with the authorities you may want to take the following precautions:

~~~~~~~~~~~~

1. Ensure your fuel outlet is not near a public institution
2. Ensure you have fire extinguishers in place
3. Ensure your infrastructure is well constructed in a way that doesn’t pose danger to the public

Please note that if you are intending to open a fully-fledged filling station that sells petrol and diesel you will have to get a certificate from ERC and NEMA to operate your business.
