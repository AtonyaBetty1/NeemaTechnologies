~Water Vending Machines~

A Water Vending Machine also known as “Water ATM” is a coin-operated water RO (Reverse Osmosis) machine that can be installed at any Public/Private place to deliver “Healthy drinking water” at most affordable prices and convenience.

The concept of water vending is simple: The customer owns his own bottles and fills them at his convenience.

Unlike machines with bottles, a water vending machine doesn't need to be checked often.

It doesn't need to be restocked. Vending machine earnings vary widely based on many different variables.

If you do it right, you can make profits for years to come.

~How the Water Vending machine works~

The coin operated drinking water vending machine is a machine which works the same way earlier telephones used to work. You have to insert the money and then you will get the water according to your amount of money.

Growing business of the Water Vending Machine in Kenya

The water vending machine business has become popular over the last few years. This is because it is such a low-cost investment.

The baby boomers seem to be making water machines more popular. These machines don’t require as much maintenance as other vending machines, so the profits can get pretty high. You will get the maximum profits from areas that are lacking safe tap water.

The best locations include hospitals, gas stations, water parks, parking lots, gyms, apartment complexes, and more.

~Things to Consider Before Starting Water vending business~

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Before you register with a company for water supply, make sure that their water is of the highest quality, research on the company offering the water, their capacity and standards and other important aspects of the supplying company before starting.

Some of the locally available water sourcing, for example, uses a reverse osmosis processing system to ensure safe, pure, great tasting water.

Also check with the local authorities about the requirement for licenses, which often tend to be very much accessible.

These machines are definitely in demand right now, and as humans beings we are always going to need water.

Maintenance of the Water Vending Machine

When drinking water is stored, it cannot simply be left in a tank to be accessed whenever someone pleases. The best water vending machines need to be cleaned and checked upon regularly.

This way, no bacteria, dirt or any other kind of substance can contaminate the water. That is why we have provided our clients with the capability of checking in on the machines.

The process only takes about 15 minutes, and involves vend testing, water quality checking, cleaning the machine, and obviously taking out the money.

We also offer a guide for how often the filters should be changed.
